================================================================================
TECHNICAL DETAILS
================================================================================

Location Encoder Architecture:
  Input: 2D normalized coordinates (latitude, longitude)
  Hidden: 32 units (embed_dim / 2)
  Output: 12-step traffic predictions
  - Direct influence on final output through fusion decoder
  
Temporal Encoder:
  Input: 12 timesteps × 2 features (flow + hour) per location
  Output: 64-dim embedding
  - Fused with location encoding for final predictions
  
Spatial-Temporal Mixer:
  3 layers (reduced to 2 for training speed)
  Each layer: Spatial mixing across nodes + Channel mixing
  - Essential for modeling temporal dependencies
  
Training Strategy:
  - Subset training: 3,564 samples (not all 17,523) for speed
  - Single epoch: 110 batches × 32 samples
  - No gradient clipping or regularization
  - Direct supervision on 12-step ahead predictions
